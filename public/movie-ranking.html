A Few Of My Favourite Movies:

My Top Movies Of All Times:

1:Taare Zameen Par

This is a hindi movie by Amir Khan and it depicts the use of creativity in teaching students to make learning effective and interactive.

2.Gandadagudi:

Really cool kannada movie by Punith Rajkumar. It depicts the nature and wildlife of Karnataka.

3.Charlie:

A must watch movie for all dog lovers. It shows the bonding between a lonely factory worker and a dog.
