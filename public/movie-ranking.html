The Best Movies According To Nandini

My Top 3 Movies Of All Times

1:THREE IDIOTS

The story follows the friendship of 3 students at an Indian engineering college and is a satire about the social pressures under the Indian education system.

2:TAARE ZAMEEN PAR

It is a film about children and it celebrates the abilities of children.

3:CHARLIE

This movie includes a message about adopting pets and importance of animal conduct.
